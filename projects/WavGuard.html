WavGuard — Smart Wakeboard

WavGuard is a smart wakeboard designed to help beginner riders improve balance and technique through real-time feedback. The board integrates pressure and tilt sensors to track stance and weight distribution, translating rider data into intuitive visual cues that guide users toward proper positioning.

Project Demo

This video demonstrates an early prototype of the WavGuard system during testing. Embedded sensors detect changes in rider stance and weight distribution, triggering LED indicators that provide immediate feedback. The demo emphasizes how subtle, real-time cues can accelerate learning without distracting the rider.

Testing was conducted in controlled conditions; the example shown here highlights how the board responds to heel/toe shifts and shows the latency and stability of the feedback loop. Future iterations will refine ergonomics and sensor placement.
