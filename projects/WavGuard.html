WavGuard — Smart Wakeboard

WavGuard is a smart wakeboard designed to help beginner riders improve balance and technique through real-time feedback. The board integrates pressure and tilt sensors to track stance and weight distribution, translating rider data into intuitive visual cues that guide users toward proper positioning.

Project Demo

This video demonstrates an early prototype of the WavGuard system during testing. Embedded sensors detect changes in rider stance and weight distribution, triggering LED indicators that provide immediate feedback.

The demo highlights how the feedback system is designed to be intuitive and minimally distracting, allowing riders to adjust posture naturally without interrupting their ride.
