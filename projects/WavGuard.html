WavGuard- Smart wakeboard
Role: Engineer · Location: Duke University · Date: Fall 2025

I spent eight weeks in Eswatini working alongside a team of Duke classmates, Engineers In Action staff, local masons, and community members to design and construct the Nyakuza Suspended Footbridge—the longest pedestrian bridge in the country at 128 meters.

Prior to traveling in-country, I assisted with the bridge’s design during the academic year, participating in engineering review calls and collaborating on structural decisions. While on-site, I worked directly with community members to ensure construction quality and safety.
