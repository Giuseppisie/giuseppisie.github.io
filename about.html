About

Hi — I’m Sienna Giuseppi, a mechanical engineer at Duke University (Class of 2028) studying Mechanical Engineering with minors in Computer Science and Visual Arts. I design hardware with a focus on user-centered consumer electronics and love solving hands-on challenges from prototyping to product delivery.

I combine rigorous engineering practice with a creative design mindset. I enjoy fast iteration, fabrication, and working across disciplines — whether that's embedded systems, PCB layout, CAD, or industrial design. I’m currently interested in product design for consumer electronics and embedded systems, and I’m always learning new tools and techniques to make better, more delightful products.
